Mills Act research

Mills Act is a tax incentive to help the owners of historic homes repair, maintain and restore their homes by reducing property taxes. Let us research the archives and produce all historic permits for your property.

Historic Cultural Monuments

Applying for Monument status requires a complete and thorough case for the significance of the property - an association with important residents, the work of an important architect, the site of an important event, or an excellent example of a particular style of architecture. HCM applications require the most complete history of all the historic designations, including research on past residents, architects, first owners, social and economic conditions that affected the property, as well as complete permit research.

Maps and Permits

By applying multiple layers of search criteria, Way Back Homes can obtain whatever permits exist, including permits for new building, demolition, mechanicals (plumbing, electrical HVAC), additions and alterations. For anyone interested in restoring their home, applying for Mills Act, nominating their home for Monument status, creating an historic district, adding on, or just to satisfy their curiosity, permit research is the place to begin.
